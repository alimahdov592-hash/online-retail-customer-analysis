# 📊 Online Retail Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This interactive Power BI dashboard analyzes an online retail dataset to uncover revenue trends, customer behavior, product performance, and return patterns.

The dashboard provides business-driven insights to support data-informed decision-making in retail operations.

---

## 💰 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- **Total Revenue:** 15.17M
- **Total Orders:** 36K
- **Total Returns:** 1,723
- **Unique Customers:** 5,817
- **Revenue Target Tracking (YTD)**
- **Rolling 3-Month Revenue Analysis**

---

## 📈 Business Insights

### 🔹 Revenue Trends
- Strong revenue growth during 2010–2011
- Rolling 3-month revenue smoothing highlights seasonal patterns

### 🔹 Product Performance
- *Swiss Chalet Tree Decoration* generated the highest revenue
- Some products show high return rates, impacting net revenue
- Revenue concentration exists among a limited number of SKUs

### 🔹 Customer Insights
- Majority of revenue generated from the United Kingdom (~96%)
- Top customers contribute significant revenue share
- Return behavior varies across customers and countries

### 🔹 Sales Behavior
- Weekly and monthly order trends reveal seasonal purchasing patterns
- Revenue distribution varies significantly by day of week

---

## 🛠 Technical Implementation

- Built using **Microsoft Power BI**
- Data modeling with calculated measures
- DAX for KPI calculations
- Interactive slicers for:
  - Year
  - Weekdays
  - Product category
- Custom revenue target comparison
- Rolling revenue calculations

## 🎯 Business Value

This dashboard can assist retail managers in:

- Revenue forecasting
- Performance tracking against targets
